About the Event

What Is TCE?

The Creators' Experience (TCE) is Akure's premier gathering for content creators, digital entrepreneurs, church media teams, and creative professionals. Each edition is themed around a transformative concept — and 2026's theme is The Blueprint.


This is not just an event — it's a movement. A space where influence is designed with intentionality and legacy is built with purpose. Whether you're just starting out or scaling your creative brand, TCE is where your blueprint takes shape.
